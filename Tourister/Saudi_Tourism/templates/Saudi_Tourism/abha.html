Abha

At an altitude of 2,200 m between the peaks of the Sarawat Mountains, Abha, is ideally located in southwestern region of Saudi Arabia.

Here some of leading destinations in Abha city

Al-Dabab Walkway

Spend some special time with the clouds and visit one of the absolutely most breathtaking destinations.

The High City

High City has an ideal strategic location at the heart of Abha since it is surrounded by many elegant hotels and captivating parks as well as it is easily reachable via King Abdulaziz Road from all parts of the city.

Fatimah’s Museum of Aseeri Woman’s Heritage

The minute you lay your eyes on Al-Qatt Al-Aseeri art in Fatimah’s Museum of Aseeri Woman’s Heritage, you will be overwhelmed with its beauty.

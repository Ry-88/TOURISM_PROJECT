Mekkah

In the past, it was called Bakkah, Umm Al-Qura, and Al-Balad Al-Amin, and its mountains witnessed the first revelation.

Here some of leading destinations in Mekkah city

Grand Mosque in Makkah

Since the dawn of civilization, the Kaaba of the Grand Mosque has had great religious significance, owing to its religious connotations associated with the Creator.

Hira Cultural District Tour

THira Cultural District, located next to the famous landmark of Makkah, Jabal Al-Nour, provides a uniquely enriching experience, in a cultural, historical and entertaining journey to the worlds of the past, to remain alive in memory.

The Kiswa Factory of the Holy Kaaba

New black, pure-silk clothing for Kaaba, called kiswa, is made by the Kiswa Factory of the Holy Kaaba (King Abdulaziz Complex) every year.
